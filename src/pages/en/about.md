## Pure IT Trends & Tech Insight Curator
I am editorNOM, a tech curator who identifies the context of complex technologies.

Have you ever felt lost amidst the flood of IT news and new technologies?
It's not easy to find the 'true meaning' of technology between tech blogs full of jargon like alien languages and soulless articles that simply copy-paste press releases.

I gather fragmented technological trends and translate the hidden context behind them into easy-to-read language.

How AI is reshaping the web ecosystem beyond daily life, why next-generation security architectures had to emerge, and what kind of future Big Tech companies in Silicon Valley are designing.

Beyond simply listing technical 'specs', I record insights on the essential question: **"So why was this technology made, and what changes will it bring?"** I welcome those who want to read the flow of technology in the most sophisticated and clear way.
